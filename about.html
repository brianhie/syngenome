About

SynGenome is a first of its kind synthetic metagenomics database

Methodology

We take DNA sequences corresponding to UniProt IDs and use them to sample with Evo.

Limitations

Sampling with a large DNA language model is prone to repetitive sequences and fundamentally these are synthetic sequences.
