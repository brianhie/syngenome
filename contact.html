Collaborations and partnerships

If you are interested in conducting a semantic mining project beyond the scale of SynGenome, please reach out as we are eager to collaborate. A design project could be a good contender for semantic mining if:

  1. Your desired function is encoded by prokaryotic genomes. These could include systems like nucleases, protein-based antimicrobials, transposons, polymerases, or metabolic enzymes. We are also interested in further exploring anti-CRISPR systems, a task for which we already have experimental validation.

  2. You have a low- to medium-throughput experimental pipeline for testing your desired function. While higher throughput is usually better, we have had experimental success rates of 14-50% screening 10-84 designs. Evo designs entire protein sequences from scratch, which can then be synthesized as gene fragments.

If you are interested in working together, please fill out this Google form to begin the conversation!
